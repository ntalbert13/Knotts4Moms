Calico Ghost Town

Walter Knott actually lived in Calico when he was a young boy, he'd work on the houses there until he was able to buy the entire town, he later then sold it to San Bernadino County while bringing some of Calico Ghost Town to Buena Park!

Old Knotts

Knotts berry farm was an actual farm that introduced the Boysenberry, While selling berries and Mrs. Knotts famous chicken, Mr. Knott had built several things to keep guests entertained while they waited, such as a beautiful fountain/garden that you can still find today inside the gift shop next to Mrs. Knotts Chicken
